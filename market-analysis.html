Market Analysis

Competitive Landscape

The workflow automation/iPaaS market is crowded and growing, with several key competitors:

Zapier

Incumbent leader with 5,000-6,000+ integrations

300k+ paying customers, ~$300-400M revenue

Known for ease of use and reliability

Make (formerly Integromat)

~1,500 app integrations

Targets more tech-savvy users

Advanced features like iterators and routers

Pipedream

~2,000 pre-built integrations

Developer-centric with code in workflows

~900k+ users, generous free tier

Workato

~1,200 connectors

Enterprise-focused with security emphasis

Higher price point, deeper enterprise features

n8n's position: Unique as an open-source alternative with more control and flexibility than SaaS-only tools, appealing to technical users who find closed-source tools too restrictive.

Market Size, Growth & Share

Market Size & Growth

  • Global workflow automation market: ~$23.8B in 2025
  • Projected to reach $37B+ by 2030
  • iPaaS market: $3.7B in 2021, forecast to reach $13.9B by 2026 (CAGR ~30%)
  • Growth driven by surge in SaaS adoption and need to connect disparate systems

n8n's Market Position

  • Newer entrant (founded 2019) with open-source model
  • 200,000+ users and 3,000+ enterprises
  • Substantial community for a young platform
  • 7× community growth in 12 months
  • Still a fraction of Zapier's multi-million user footprint

Monetization Strategy

n8n's Model

  • Freemium open-core model
  • Core software is free and source-available
  • Revenue from Cloud hosting plans and enterprise offerings
  • Charges based on workflow executions per month
  • Starting ~€20/month for 2,500 executions
  • Self-hosting option at near-zero marginal cost

Competitor Models

  • Zapier: Tiered subscriptions with pay-per-action pricing
  • Make: Subscription plans based on operation counts
  • Pipedream: Free tier with usage fees for expanded workflows
  • Workato: Enterprise SaaS subscriptions with annual contracts

Cost Comparison Example

Service Cost
100,000 actions on Zapier ~$734/month
Equivalent on n8n Cloud ~$80-$100/month
Self-hosted n8n Server costs only

Key differentiator: n8n charges per workflow execution, not per step/action, making it significantly more cost-effective at scale.
